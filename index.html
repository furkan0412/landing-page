Transistors are awesome

Transistors are semiconductor devices that act as electronic switches or amplifiers, controlling the flow of electrical current in circuits. They are fundamental components in modern electronics, enabling the functionality of computers, smartphones, and other devices.

Different Types of Transistors

A BJT (Bipolar Junction Transistor) is a three-terminal semiconductor device used for amplification or switching in electronic circuits. It consists of three regions: emitter, base, and collector, formed by doping a semiconductor material to create NPN or PNP configurations.

A FET (Field-Effect Transistor) is a three-terminal semiconductor device used for switching and amplification in electronic circuits. It consists of a source, gate, and drain, with current flow controlled by an electric field created by the voltage applied to the gate.

An IGBT (Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistor) is a three-terminal power semiconductor device combining the high input impedance of a MOSFET with the low on-state power loss of a BJT.
